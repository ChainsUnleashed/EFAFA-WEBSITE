Leaders of the Past Present Future

The EFAFA is the grouping of former students of the school Frère Adrien du Sacre-Coeur. This group of students is an independent movement that does not commit to the school establishment properly called Frère Adrien Ecole du Sacre-Coeur. It is a voluntary movement whose goal is to provide support and assistance to that said establishment and Haitian education.

Mission Statement

  1. To serve and promote the educational and recreational.
  2. To serve as an educational forum for alumni to maintain contact with the school and each other.
  3. To support periodic reunions and other events for alumni and students.
  4. To carry out fundraising and other charitable activities for the benefit of the school, its current students and alumni.
  5. To function as the Alumni official voice in communicating with the school.
